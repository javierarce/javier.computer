About this and that

Hi, stranger, welcome to my little computer on the net!

This website contains a wiki, several blog posts, a collection of silly songs, a pun, a hidden passage to another dimension, a ghost story, a list of dog names, a list of names that are not dog names, references to my early memories, several photos of myself eating ice cream, a mirror, a coin, a yellow bird, a photo of your turtle, a turtle.

I get sad when I only spend my time consuming instead of making things. This website is my attempt to spend more time thinking, documenting, and making.

If you like what you are reading, add this RSS feed to your favorite feed reader to get the updates. And if you need to contact me and tell me something incredible, use this link.
