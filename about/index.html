About me

  • My name is Javier Arce, comme tout le monde.
  • I like to write, draw, read, photography and do silly things on the Internet. My vital goal is to retire to do this all the time.
  • I work as a designer at Automattic. Before that I was paid to code (CARTO, Vizzuality).
  • In my time as a programmer, I blocked the security system of the site where I worked to get several dinosaur embryos, which caused the perimeter fences to lose power and wait because I think I have described something that happens in Jurassic Park.
  • I have not eaten meat since the beginning of 2017, but I cannot consider myself completely vegetarian because I occasionally eat fish.
  • I lived in Amsterdam for two rainy years and another four in Barcelona.
  • I would like to live in Berlin.
  • My favorite author is Georges Perec.
  • The movie I have seen the most times is Children of Men.
  • I am unable to read the time on an analog clock at a reasonable speed.
  • I was born in León.
  • I have been living in Madrid for 8 years.
  • I have kept a playlist for 10 years where I keep my favorite songs.
  • I studied German for three years and spent one in Frankfurt as Erasmus.
  • A few years back I worked designing book covers.

  • I'm interested in experimental and folk games, book covers, and zines.
  • For the past three years I have organized a festival of computational literature called Poetry Slash. In 2020, for obvious and pandemic reasons, the event was canceled.
